* **Slide Line:** Instant & Automated Legal Support
* **Demo Explanation:** "When an SOS is triggered, the app automatically switches to Evidence Capture Mode. It instantly starts recording audio, captures camera/screenshot evidence, and securely logs the exact location. This ensures crucial data is preserved even if the phone is compromised."
* **Judge Answer (If asked "Why?"):** "In a state of panic, a user cannot manually open a camera or voice recorder. Automating evidence collection provides immense legal support and emotional empowerment."

### 3. 📍 Geo-Fencing Alerts & Live Guardian Mode
* **Slide Line:** Auto-Triggered Safety Corridors
* **Demo Explanation:** "We are moving from reactive to proactive safety. With Geo-Fencing, if a user enters a known high-risk zone, the app instantly alerts them and auto-activates Live Guardian tracking. It actively broadcasts their real-time location and status to their trusted circle."
* **Judge Answer (If asked "Why?"):** "It removes the friction of manually activating tracking when entering a dangerous area, ensuring nobody is ever left alone in a crisis."

### 4. 🧬 Holistic AI Risk Prediction Score
* **Slide Line:** Unified Safety & Wellness Snapshot
