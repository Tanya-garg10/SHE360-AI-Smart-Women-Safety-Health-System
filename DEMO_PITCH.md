# 🏆 SHE360 AI - Demo & Pitch Guide

This document contains the exact high-impact lines and strategy to present the newly added features to judges during your demo or PPT.

## 🎯 The Core Strategy
**Don't say:** *"I added many features to this app."*
**Say:** *"These features solve real-world scenarios like proactive threat detection and holistic wellness tracking."*

## 🔥 The "Winning Combo" Features Added

### 1. 🧠 AI Risk Prediction Score
* **Location in App:** Dashboard (Top prominent section)
* **What it does:** Calculates a real-time combined score based on the user's Safety readiness, Health assessments, and Mental wellness trends.
* **Line to say during Demo/PPT:**
  > *"Instead of showing scattered data, our system calculates a real-time, unified Risk Score using AI models across Safety, Health, and Mental Wellness to give the user a complete holistic snapshot."*

### 2. 📍 Geo-Fencing Alerts
* **Location in App:** Safety Hub (Use the new "SIMULATE ZONE" button on the map)
* **What it does:** Warns the user instantly if they enter a known unsafe or low-light zone, and automatically triggers tracking.
* **Line to say during Demo/PPT:**
  > *"We are moving from reactive to proactive safety. With Geo-Fencing, if a user enters a known low-light or high-risk zone, the app instantly alerts them and auto-activates Live Guardian tracking."*

### 3. 👥 Live Guardian Mode
* **Location in App:** Safety Hub (Map section "GO LIVE" / "STOP LIVE" buttons)
* **What it does:** Allows the user to actively broadcast their live location and status to their trusted circle.
* **Line to say during Demo/PPT:**
  > *"When an alert is triggered, Live Guardian Mode automatically broadcasts the user's real-time location and status directly to their trusted contacts' dashboard, ensuring nobody is ever left alone in a crisis."*

---

## 💥 Bonus Line (Perfect for the Intro or Conclusion of the PPT)
> **"Our system not only reacts to emergencies but also predicts risks and alerts users in advance."**

### 💡 Demo Flow Tips
1. Start at the **Dashboard** -> Show the *AI Risk Prediction Score*.
2. Go to **Safety Hub** -> Click *SIMULATE ZONE* to show the dramatic red Geo-Fence Alert pop-up.
3. Show how the alert automatically triggers *Live Guardian Mode* to "BROADCASTING LIVE".
